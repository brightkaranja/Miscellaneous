Quality analysis

It is the identifing of a problem, generating corrective action, verifing them and implimenting corrective action.

There are different types Quality Assurance:

  1. Preventive - It prevents defects and errors from occuring.
  2. Detective - Identifing and fixing errors occuring.
  3. Corrective - Fixing problems that arise during and after production.
  4. Assessment - Ensure the consistency and reliability of a product.
